Camping at Big Lodge Sur

Big Sur has several campground that are available. Some of the campground had beautiful cabins but we opted out for camping in a tent. Big Lodge Sur was one of the bigger campground and has plenty of spots to pick from. Few things to remember about Big Sur: the bears are very real and cell service is nonexistent.
